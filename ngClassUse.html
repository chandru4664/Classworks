In a move that is certain to spread cheer among city residents,
Chennai Metro Rail Limited (CMRL) has started returning parks it
had acquired for construction. It will also fund their restoration.
It has already handed over a park in Ashok Nagar to the city corporation.
A corporation official said they had started restoration work last week.
"Metro rail has asked us to prepare an estimate so that it can hand over the money.
The restoration is expected to be completed in three months at a cost of 40 lakh."
